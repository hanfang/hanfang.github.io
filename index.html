Han Fang

Welcome to Han Fang's GitHub Pages.

Han Fang received his M.S. in Applied Math & Statistics at Stony Brook University (SBU) and received his B.S. in Optical Informatics at Sun Yat-sen University. He is currently supervised by Dr. Gholson Lyon and Dr. Michael Schatz at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory. He is interested in genomics, human genetics and algorithm development.
